HelmNative is a native macOS application built with SwiftUI. This guide will help you get the project running in Xcode for development.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have the following installed on your Mac:

- **macOS 14.0** or newer
- **Xcode 16.0** or newer
- **Git**

## Setup Flow

Follow these steps to set up HelmNative for local development.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Clone the Repository" icon="git-branch">
    Clone the project from GitHub and navigate to the project root:
    ```bash
    git clone git@github.com:clientamp/helmnative.git
    cd helmnative
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open in Xcode" icon="code-2">
    Open the Xcode project file:
    ```bash
    open HelmNative.xcodeproj
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Package Resolution" icon="package">
    Xcode will automatically resolve the **HelmCore** local package dependency located in the `Packages/HelmCore/` directory. Wait for the resolution process to complete.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Target" icon="layout-template">
    Select the **HelmMac** target from the scheme selector at the top of the Xcode window. Choose **My Mac** as the destination.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run" icon="zap">
    Press **âŒ˜ + R** or click the **Product > Run** menu to build and launch HelmNative.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Authentication & IMAP

Once the app is running, you can connect your existing email accounts:

1.  Navigate to **Settings > Accounts**.
2.  Click **Add Account**.
3.  Enter your **IMAP and SMTP** details.
4.  HelmNative will perform a direct sync with your mail server. No intermediate servers are used.

<Callout kind="warning" title="Privacy Note">
  HelmNative stores your email account credentials securely in the **macOS Keychain**. Your passwords are never transmitted to anyone other than your email provider.
</Callout>

## AI Integration

To use the AI-powered classification and drafting features, you need to configure an AI provider:

1.  Navigate to **Settings > AI**.
2.  Select your preferred provider (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, xAI, or Ollama).
3.  Enter your **API Key**.

<Callout kind="tip" title="Using Ollama">
  For a completely local experience, you can select **Ollama** and point HelmNative to your local Ollama instance (typically `http://localhost:11434`).
</Callout>

## Troubleshooting

- **Database Errors**: If you encounter schema errors, you can reset the local database by deleting the SQLite files in your user's `Library/Application Support/HelmNative` directory.
- **Keychain Access**: Ensure you grant the app permission to access the macOS Keychain when prompted.
