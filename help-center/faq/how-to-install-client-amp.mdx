## Overview

Client Amp is Lickity Click's tracking script. Add it to your site to log user actions.

## Quick Install

1. Sign in to your Lickity Click account.
2. Go to **Client Amp > Setup**.
3. Copy your unique script tag.

```html
<script src="https://cdn.lickityclick.com/client-amp.js" data-key="YOUR_KEY"></script>
```

4. Paste just before your site's `</body>` tag.

You're set!
