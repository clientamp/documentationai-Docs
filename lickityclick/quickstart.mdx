## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info" title="What you'll need">
- A free account (no credit card required)
- Any long URL you want to shorten
- 2 minutes of your time
</Callout>

No prior setup or technical knowledge needed. Lickity Click works in your browser instantly.

## Create your first short link

Follow these steps to go from signup to tracking clicks.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up for free" icon="user-plus">
    Visit [https://app.lickity.click/signup](https://app.lickity.click/signup) and create your account.

    No credit card or payment details required. Use your email or social login.

    <Image
      src="https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1556757347-88f985b64761?w=800"
      alt="Signup form on Lickity Click dashboard"
      width="800"
      height="500"
    />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Paste your URL" icon="link">
    In the dashboard, paste any long URL into the input field.

````markdown
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ&list=example-playlist
````

    Click **Shorten** to generate your branded short link.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Customize the slug" icon="edit-3">
    Edit the slug for a custom short name like `lickity.click/join` or `yourdomain.link/podcast`.

    Add UTM parameters if needed:

    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Manual UTM" icon="hash">
````javascript
https://lickity.click/join?utm_source=youtube&utm_medium=description&utm_campaign=podcast-launch
````
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="UTM Builder" icon="settings">
        Use the built-in UTM generator in the dashboard—no code required.
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Share your link" icon="share-2">
    Copy the short link and share it across platforms.

    <Columns cols={3}>
      <Card title="Social" icon="twitter" href="#">
        Post on X, Instagram, or TikTok.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Email" icon="mail" href="#">
        Add to newsletters or signatures.
      </Card>
      <Card title="QR Code" icon="qrcode" href="#">
        Download the instant QR code PNG.
      </Card>
    </Columns>
  </Step>

  <Step title="View real-time analytics" icon="bar-chart-3">
    Return to your dashboard. Clicks update instantly—no waiting for reports.

    | Metric       | Description                  |
    |--------------|------------------------------|
    | Total Clicks | All clicks on the link       |
    | Top Country  | Most common visitor location |
    | Device Type  | Mobile, desktop, or tablet   |
    | Referrers    | Where clicks came from       |

    <Callout kind="success">
      First click appears in under 1 second!
    </Callout>
  </Step>
</Steps>

  **Link not shortening?** Check URL format—no spaces or invalid characters.

  **No analytics showing?** Share the link publicly first. Private tests may not track.

  **Custom domain issues?** Verify your DNS CNAME record points to `lickity.click`.
