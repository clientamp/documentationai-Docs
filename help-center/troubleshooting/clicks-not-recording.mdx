---
title: Clicks Not Recording
description: What to do if clicks aren't showing in the dashboard.
---

## Symptoms

- No click data in Lickity Click dashboard
- No network requests to `/track`

## Possible Causes

- Script not loaded
- Ad blocker interference

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Verify script presence">
    ````html
    <script src="https://cdn.lickityclick.com/client-amp.js"></script>
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check browser console">
    Look for errors related to client-amp.js
  </Step>
  <Step title="Whitelist your site">
    Ask users to disable ad blockers for testing.
  </Step>
</Steps>
