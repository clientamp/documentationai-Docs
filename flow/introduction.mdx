Flow is a productivity application designed to provide a calm and focused environment for managing your daily tasks and long-term projects. Deeply inspired by the minimalist philosophy of Things 3, Flow combines a beautiful, native-feeling UI with powerful features like natural language date parsing, realtime collaboration, and deep keyboard navigation.

## Clarity by Design

Flow is structured to help you organize your life with ease, moving from high-level responsibilities to minute details.

- **Areas & Projects**: Organize your work into broad Areas of Responsibility (e.g., "Work", "Personal", "Health") and specific, actionable Projects.
- **Headings & Tasks**: Break down complex projects into logical sections using Headings, and track your progress with granular Tasks and checklists.
- **Inbox & Today**: Quickly capture ideas in the Inbox and plan your daily focus using the Today and Upcoming views.

## Built for Speed

Flow is designed for a keyboard-centric workflow, ensuring you can manage your tasks without ever leaving your home row.

## Tech Stack

Flow uses a modern, reactive stack designed for performance and scale:

- **Framework**: Next.js 16 (App Router, TypeScript)
- **Database**: **Supabase** (PostgreSQL, Auth, and Realtime)
- **State**: **Zustand** client-side stores for blazing-fast UI updates
- **Editor**: **Tiptap** for rich-text task notes
- **Animation**: Framer Motion for a fluid, native-feeling experience
