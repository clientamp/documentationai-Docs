HelmNative reimagines the email client as a local, private workspace for communication and task management.

## Direct IMAP Sync

HelmNative connects directly from your Mac to your email provider using high-performance **native IMAP** connections via `Network.framework`.

- **No Middle-man Servers**: Your emails never touch a third-party cloud.
- **Background Syncing**: Email accounts are synchronized in the background, ensuring your inbox is always up to date.
- **ValueObservation Persistence**: Using **GRDB.swift**, the app efficiently monitors changes to the local SQLite database and updates the UI in real-time.

## AI Classification

HelmNative automatically analyzes incoming emails to help you focus on what matters most.

- **Categories**: Emails are intelligently labeled as **Personal**, **Newsletter**, or **Transactional**.
- **Priority**: AI sets a priority level for each message, ensuring critical items are highlighted.
- **Smart Labels**: AI can apply custom labels based on email content, like "Invoice", "Meeting Request", or "Support".

<Callout kind="info" title="Privacy-First AI">
  HelmNative sends email content to your chosen AI provider (e.g., Anthropic, OpenAI, or local Ollama) for analysis, but your account credentials and full mailbox are never shared.
</Callout>

## Fishnet Screening

**Fishnet** is a powerful AI-first screening tool that protects your inbox from unknown senders and spam.

1.  **Unknown Sender Protection**: When an email from a new sender arrives, it is held in the Fishnet queue.
2.  **AI Verdict**: AI evaluates the message and provides a "verdict" based on its likely value and risk.
3.  **Human Review**: You can quickly review the Fishnet queue and choose to **Allow** or **Block** senders for future messages.

<Callout kind="success" title="Inbox Zero">
  Fishnet significantly reduces the noise in your primary inbox by ensuring only trusted or valuable senders make it through.
</Callout>

## Smart Drafts & Voice Guides

HelmNative's **Draft Generator** helps you write faster by creating draft responses based on your personal style and existing email context.

### Voice Guides
Create your own **Voice Guides** to teach the AI how you want to sound.
- **Tone**: Specify if you should be formal, friendly, or direct.
- **Context**: Provide examples of your preferred phrasing and common responses.
- **Consistency**: Use different Voice Guides for different email accounts (e.g., Professional vs. Personal).

## Tech Architecture

HelmNative is built on a modular package architecture:

- **HelmCore**: A shared Swift package containing all business logic, data models (`TodoItem`, `EmailMessage`), and AI services.
- **HelmMac**: The native macOS application target containing the SwiftUI views and app-specific lifecycle logic.
- **Database Architecture**: Persistence is handled by **GRDB.swift**, providing a reactive and performant SQLite layer with FTS5 for lightning-fast search.
