Sponsor CRM provides a centralized, intelligent workspace for managing the entire lifecycle of your creator sponsorships.

## Sponsor Management & Tiers

Stay organized as your sponsor list grows with intelligent grouping and tiering.

- **Tiered Organization**: Automatically or manually classify sponsors into tiers (e.g., Platinum, Gold, Silver) based on contract value, relationship history, and strategic importance.
- **Detailed Metadata**: Store essential contact information, brand guidelines, preferred communication styles, and historical deal notes for every sponsor.
- **AI Classification**: (Planned) Leverages AI to suggest sponsor tiers based on historical performance and interaction frequency.

## Deal Pipeline

Visualize and manage your active sponsorships through every stage of the funnel.

- **Stage Tracking**: Track deals from **Discovery** and **Outreach** to **Contract Signed**, **Content Delivered**, and **Invoiced**.
- **Drag-and-Drop View**: A flexible Kanban-style pipeline view for quickly moving deals through their lifecycle.
- **Value Projections**: See the aggregate value of your pipeline at each stage to help with financial planning.

<Callout kind="info" title="Deal Stages">
  Default stages include: Identified, Contacted, Negotiating, Contract Sent, Signed, In Production, Live, and Paid.
</Callout>

## Outreach Automation

Streamline your communication with brands and agencies.

- **Follow-up Reminders**: Set automatic reminders for when a sponsor hasn't replied to an outreach message.
- **Communication History**: View a unified history of every email and deal interaction with a specific brand.
- **Auto-Drafted Responses**: Integrated AI helps you generate outreach messages and reply drafts that are customized to the specific brand and deal context.

## Performance Reporting

Prove the value of your audience with real-time data integration.

- **LickityClick Integration**: Sponsor CRM connects directly to your **LickityClick** account.
- **Real-time Metrics**: Surface click performance, conversion rates, and ROI metrics directly on the sponsor detail page.
- **Shareable Summaries**: Generate professional performance summaries to send to your sponsors alongside your final content reports.

<Callout kind="success" title="Build Trust">
  Proving ROI with hard data is the most effective way to ensure sponsors renew their deals.
</Callout>

## Helm Native Integration

Deep email synchronization ensures your CRM is always up to date.

- **Unified Inbox**: Pull in sponsor-related emails directly from your **Helm Native** workspace.
- **Contextual View**: See all relevant emails for a specific deal right next to its pipeline status and metadata.
- **Smart Labels**: AI-powered labeling ensures sponsor outreach is never buried in your general inbox.
