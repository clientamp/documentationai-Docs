## Overview

Lickity Click lets you create branded short links with real-time analytics. Track every click from your audience, monitor countries, devices, and referrers. Prove your ROI on sponsor deals with detailed reports and conversion tracking.

You get custom domains, QR codes, smart redirects, and link health monitoring. Start free with no card required. Built for creators who need simple, powerful link management.
