Sponsor CRM is a professional, creator-focused platform designed to streamline the lifecycle of brand sponsorships. Built with **Next.js** and **PostgreSQL**, it provides a centralized workspace for organizing sponsor tiers, managing active deals, and proving value through integrated performance reporting.

## Creator-First Deal Management

Unlike generic CRMs, Sponsor CRM is built specifically for the workflows of modern creators.

- **Deal Pipeline**: Visualize your sponsorship funnel from initial outreach to contract signed and content delivered.
- **Outreach Automation**: Track follow-ups and communication history to ensure no potential deal falls through the cracks.
- **Sponsor Tiering**: Automatically classify sponsors into tiers based on value, relationship length, and engagement levels.

## Tech Stack

Modern, performant, and type-safe:

- **Framework**: Next.js (App Router)
- **Database**: PostgreSQL with **Drizzle ORM**
- **Auth**: Integrated secure authentication
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS & Radix UI
