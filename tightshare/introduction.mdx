TightShare (also known as **PostFlow**) is a native macOS application designed for content creators who want to maximize the value of their video content. It automates the complex process of transforming YouTube videos into fully-formatted, SEO-optimized blog posts, complete with tracked affiliate links and social media content.

## Content Transformation

TightShare's primary workflow allows you to take any YouTube video and generate a comprehensive set of publishing-ready assets:

- **Blog Post Generation**: Automatically converts your video transcript into a structured, readable blog post for platforms like **Ghost CMS**.
- **Metadata Optimization**: Generates optimized titles, descriptions, and tags to ensure your content reaches its full audience.
- **Social Content**: Creates a variety of social media snippets for Twitter, Instagram, and newsletters based on your video's key takeaways.

## Integration & Tracking

- **LickityClick Integration**: Automatically generate and embed tracked affiliate links using the LickityClick API, allowing you to monitor conversions from your blog posts.
- **Bring Your Own Key (BYOK)**: Connect your own **Anthropic API** key to power the AI orchestration, ensuring you only pay for the AI processing you use.
- **Direct Distribution**: TightShare is distributed as a notarized macOS `.dmg` for a fast, native installation process.

## Tech Stack

Built for the future of macOS automation:

- **UI**: SwiftUI (macOS 14+)
- **Logic**: Swift 6 with strict concurrency
- **Persistence**: **SwiftData** for local-first project management
- **Security**: macOS **Keychain** for secure storage of CMS and AI credentials
- **Integrations**: Ghost REST API, Anthropic API, LickityClick API
