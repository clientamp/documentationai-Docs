TightEdit transforms raw recordings into polished rough cuts automatically. It sits between capture and full NLE polish—handling the tedious work of cutting silence, removing bad takes, and creating timeline-ready edits.

## Executive Summary

- **Text-based editing**: Edit transcript = edit video.
- **Automatic rough cuts**: AI removes silence, filler words, and repetitions.
- **Command architecture**: Every action is undoable, recordable, and AI-controllable.
- **Caption burn-in**: Professional social media captions with word-by-word highlighting.

## Development Philosophy: Apple-First

We leverage native Apple APIs wherever possible for optimal performance and system integration:
- **AVFoundation & Metal**: Core video processing and compositing.
- **VideoToolbox**: Hardware-accelerated encoding/decoding.
- **Core ML**: On-device AI (WhisperKit, Silero VAD).
- **SwiftUI + AppKit**: Native macOS interface.

## Technical Foundation

TightEdit is built with a decoupled service architecture:
- **TightEditingManager**: Orchestrates clip operations and timeline state.
- **CompositionBuilder**: Generates `AVComposition` for the player and exporter.
- **MetalCompositor**: Custom Metal-based video compositor for PiP and captions.
