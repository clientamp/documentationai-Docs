  ## What's New
  
  ### Core Editing Improvements
  - **Ultra-Clean Cuts**: Implemented automatic merging of adjacent cuts and sample-rate alignment to eliminate audio artifacts at edit points.
  - **Transcript Editing**: You can now delete transcript text directly to remove the corresponding video segments from the timeline.
  - **Scissor/Cut Tool**: Added a dedicated blade tool (B) and quick-cut shortcut (âŒ˜B) for manual timeline editing.

  - **Real-Time Slider Preview**: Timeline cuts now update in real-time as you adjust the silence detection sliders.

  ### Magnetic & Tight Mode
  - **Tight Mode Refactor**: Renamed "Magnetic" mode to **Tight Mode** to align with our product vision.
  - **Gap Behavior**: Fixed behavior in Tight Mode to ensure all gaps are automatically closed when clips are deleted.
  - **Mode Switching**: Improved the transition between Freeform and Tight Mode, ensuring clips reposition correctly.

  ## Initial Release
  
  TightEdit is now live as a native macOS video editor! Our first version includes:
  - Text-based video editing from transcripts.
  - High-performance on-device transcription with **WhisperKit**.
  - **Core ML** Voice Activity Detection for auto-silence removal.
  - Repetition and bad take detection.
  - Multi-cam audio synchronization.
  - Metal-accelerated virtualized timeline.
  - Professional hardware-accelerated export (MP4, HEVC, FCPXML).
