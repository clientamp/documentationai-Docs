Sponsorship Pipeline is the central system for managing brand relationships and sponsorship deals across the Dave Swift brand ecosystem. This module ensures every deal is tracked from initial outreach to final delivery and invoicing.

## Key Areas

- **Outreach**: Automated and manual sponsor outreach with proven templates.
- **Deal Tracking**: Kanban-style pipeline for managing deal stages.
- **Reporting**: Metrics and performance reports for sponsors using LickityClick data.
- **Invoicing**: Simplified invoicing and payment tracking for completed deals.

## Why Sponsorship Pipeline?

- **Brand Relationships**: Long-term partner management and communication history.
- **Professionalism**: Clear reporting and timely delivery of sponsored content.
- **Integration**: Linked with Sponsor CRM and LickityClick for seamless operations.
