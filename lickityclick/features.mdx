LickityClick provides a powerful set of tools for managing, tracking, and optimizing your short links.

## Links & UTM Builder

The core of LickityClick is its intelligent link creation workflow. When you create a new link, you can pick your preferred short domain and customize the slug.

### Intelligent UTM Builder
Instead of manually concatenating strings, use the built-in UTM builder to add `utm_source`, `utm_medium`, `utm_campaign`, `utm_content`, and `utm_term` to your destination URLs.
- **Real-time Preview**: See exactly how your final destination URL will look before you save.
- **Consistent Tags**: Ensure your campaign tracking is consistent across all links.

<Callout kind="tip">
  If you leave the `short code` blank, LickityClick will automatically generate a random string for you.
</Callout>

## Campaigns & Tags

LickityClick helps you stay organized as your link library grows.

### Campaigns
Group related links together into a single Campaign. This is perfect for tracking performance across a specific sponsor deal or product launch.
- **Aggregate Stats**: See the combined click performance for all links in a campaign.
- **Sponsor Metadata**: Store sponsor names, emails, and campaign notes directly with the link group.
- **Date Ranges**: Define start and end dates for your campaigns to monitor performance over time.

### Tags
Use tags to categorize links across different campaigns. LickityClick syncs these tags with Shlink, allowing for advanced filtering and per-tag analytics.

## Real-time Analytics

Every click on a LickityClick short URL is tracked in real-time. The analytics dashboard provides several breakdown views:

- **Clicks Over Time**: See precisely when your audience is most active.
- **Geo-location**: Interactive world map showing the top 10 countries and total country breakdown.
- **Device & Browser**: Pie charts for device types (Mobile, Desktop, Tablet, Bot) and top browser versions.
- **Referrers**: Identify which platforms (YouTube, Twitter, Newsletters) are driving the most traffic.

<Callout kind="info" title="Privacy Note">
  LickityClick respects user privacy and does not track personally identifiable information (PII). All analytics are aggregated and anonymized.
</Callout>

## QR Code Generation

Generate high-resolution QR codes for any short link instantly.
- **Configurable Size**: Adjust from 150px to 500px to fit your design needs.
- **Downloadable PNG**: Perfect for print materials, video overlays, or presentation slides.
- **Dynamic**: Since the QR code points to your short link, you can change the long URL destination anytime without reprint.

## Redirect Rules

Create smart, conditional routing for your links using Redirect Rules.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Device-based Routing" icon="smartphone">
    Redirect iOS users to the App Store and Android users to the Play Store using a single link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Language-based Routing" icon="languages">
    Send visitors to the correct localized version of your site based on their browser's language.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Query Parameter Routing" icon="code-2">
    Route users based on incoming query strings, perfect for advanced A/B testing or affiliate attribution.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Bulk Operations

Efficiency is built-in. Use the checkbox selection on the links table to perform actions on multiple links at once:
- **Bulk Tag**: Apply one or more tags to dozens of links in one go.
- **Bulk Delete**: Safely remove multiple links after a confirmation.
- **Bulk Recheck**: Manually trigger health checks for a selection of links.

## Public Reports

The **Public Reports** feature allows you to create a dedicated, shareable page for your sponsors.
- **Shareable URLs**: Reports live at `/r/[unique-slug]`.
- **Customizable**: Choose which chart sections to include (Geo, Devices, Referrers) and add custom notes for your partners.
- **Live Data**: Reports update in real-time as new clicks come in.

<Callout kind="success">
  Sponsors love seeing the data for themselves. Public reports build trust and prove the value of your audience.
</Callout>
