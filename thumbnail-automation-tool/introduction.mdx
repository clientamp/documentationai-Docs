The **Thumbnail Automation Tool** is a specialized platform designed for generating high-performance YouTube thumbnails. By combining proven visual archetypes, automated compositing, and human review, it provides a standardized framework for maximizing Click-Through Rate (CTR) and maintaining brand consistency.

## Visual Strategies

The tool is built on a "Playbook" of six core archetypes that have been proven to perform on YouTube:

- **Talking-Head Reaction**: High emotional connection through "mirroring".
- **Software/UI Close-Up**: Establishes credibility and utility for tutorials.
- **Comparison Split**: Creates immediate "A vs B" tension.
- **Before/After**: Demonstrates transformation and value.
- **Object-Led Curiosity**: Focuses on a single, high-value item.
- **Process/Workflow Reveal**: Signals deep value and expertise.

## Technical Foundation

The platform is designed for a robust, performant workflow:

- **Framework**: **Next.js 16** (App Router, TypeScript)
- **Database**: **PostgreSQL** (Drizzle ORM) for job tracking and performance data
- **Compositing**: Automated image layering and text overlay
- **Analytics**: Performance-memory layer for tracking CTR and retention data
