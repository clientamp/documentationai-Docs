Ghost Content is the central engine for all written content on daveswift.com. This module manages the production and distribution of articles, newsletters, and exclusive premium content for Ghost members.

## Key Areas

- **Blog Posts**: In-depth articles and tutorials with rich media and clear formatting.
- **Newsletters**: Regular editions for 'Dave's Toolbox' and 'New from Dave'.
- **Premium Content**: Exclusive guides and resources for paying members.
- **Course Content**: Drafts and assets for premium video courses.

## Why Ghost Content?

- **Member Value**: Consistent, high-quality content that keeps members engaged.
- **Templating**: Pre-designed newsletter layouts for rapid distribution.
- **Static-First**: Optimized for performance and readability across devices.
