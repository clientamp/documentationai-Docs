Consistent, professional communication is critical for building long-term sponsor relationships. Use these templates as a starting point, but always customize them to reflect a genuine experience with the product.

## 1. Initial Outreach
*Use this for first-time contact with a brand.*

**Goal**: Secure a first response and establish a "showcase, not review" angle.

```text
Subject: {{SPONSOR_PRODUCT}} + daveswift.com

Hi {{CONTACT_FIRST_NAME}},

I'm Dave Swift. I run daveswift.com, where I write about the tools and hardware I actually use for development, self-hosting, and content creation. My audience is mostly software engineers, DevOps folks, and tech-curious professionals who build things.

I've been using {{SPONSOR_PRODUCT}} for {{USE_CASE_DESCRIPTION}}. It's become part of my workflow, and I think there's a good fit for a sponsored showcase on my site.

Here's what I'm thinking: a dedicated post walking through how I use {{SPONSOR_PRODUCT}} in my setup, what works, and where it fits. Not a review, not a sales pitch. Just an honest walkthrough, the same way I cover everything else on the site.

Some quick numbers:

- {{MONTHLY_VISITORS}} monthly visitors
- {{EMAIL_SUBSCRIBERS}} newsletter subscribers
- {{AUDIENCE_DEMO_POINT}}

If that sounds interesting, I'm happy to chat about what a partnership could look like.

Dave
```

## 2. Follow-Up
*Use this 5-7 days after the initial pitch if you haven't heard back.*

**Goal**: Gently nudge the contact without being pushy.

```text
Subject: Re: {{SPONSOR_PRODUCT}} + daveswift.com

Hi {{CONTACT_FIRST_NAME}},

Just circling back on this. I know inboxes get buried.

The short version: I use {{SPONSOR_PRODUCT}} and I'd like to do a sponsored showcase on daveswift.com. Not a paid review. A walkthrough of how I actually use it.

If the timing's off, no worries at all. If it's a good fit, let me know and I'll send over specifics.

Dave
```

---

## 3. Deal Confirmation
*Use this after a verbal or email agreement on terms.*

**Goal**: Lock in the scope, price, and timeline before starting work.

**Key Points to Include**:
- **Deliverable**: (e.g., 1 blog post + 1 newsletter shoutout)
- **Timeline**: Draft due by {{DATE}}, Publish on {{DATE}}
- **Rate**: ${{AMOUNT}} (USD)
- **Payment Terms**: (e.g., Net 30 after publication)

---

## Guidelines for Sponsors

### "Showcase, not Review"
Dave Swift does not do "sponsored reviews." We only accept sponsorships for products Dave genuinely uses and recommends. The content is always a **walkthrough** or **showcase** of real utility, not a subjective evaluation for hire.

### Disclosure
All sponsored content must include a clear and conspicuous disclosure at the beginning of the post or video, following FTC guidelines.
