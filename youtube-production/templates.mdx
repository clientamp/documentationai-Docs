## Guidelines

### 1. Voice & Tone
- **First person**: Always use "I", "me", "my".
- **Direct**: No "fluff" or hype. State the value immediately.
- **Natural phrases**: Use "here's the thing", "let's go ahead and", "I'll be honest".
- **No emojis or em dashes**: Keep the text clean and direct.

### 2. Chapters & SEO
- **Always include timestamps**: Start with `0:00`.
- **Keyword integration**: Naturally include product or tool names in the intro and chapter titles.
- **Front-loading**: Put the most important information in the first 150 characters.

### 3. Affiliate & Sponsor Disclosure
- **Mandatory disclosure**: Always include the affiliate disclosure paragraph if using tracked links.
- **Honesty**: Only recommend products actually used in the workflow.
- **Showcase, not Review**: For sponsored segments, clearly label them as a showcase.
