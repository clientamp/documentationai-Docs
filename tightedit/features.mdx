TightEdit provides a powerful suite of native tools designed for rapid video content creation.

## Text-Based Editing

The most powerful feature of TightEdit is its text-based editing workflow. By treating your video like a document, you can edit your footage faster than ever before.

- **Transcript Deletion**: Select any text in the transcript and press **Delete**. The corresponding video is instantly removed from the timeline, and the rest of the clips ripple to close the gap.
- **Visual Feedback**: As you move through the transcript, the playhead follows in the timeline, ensuring you always have visual context for your text-based edits.

<Callout kind="success" title="Edit Faster">
  You can cut a 10-minute video in under 2 minutes by simply scanning the text for mistakes and deleting them.
</Callout>

## Auto-Silence Removal

TightEdit uses **Core ML Voice Activity Detection (VAD)** to identify silent regions in your recordings.

- **Smart Cutting**: Silence is automatically flagged for removal. You can review and adjust these cuts before they are applied.
- **Tightness Controls**: Adjust the **Minimum Silence Duration** and **Padding** sliders to control how aggressive the auto-cutting should be.
- **Ultra-Clean Cuts**: TightEdit merges adjacent cuts and aligns them with audio sample rates to eliminate pops and clicks at edit points.

## Bad Take Detection

Using **Repetition Analysis**, TightEdit can identify where you've repeated phrases or had "bad takes."

- **Automatic Grouping**: Repeats are grouped together, and the AI suggests which take is the best one to keep.
- **One-Click Cleanup**: Quickly remove all but the final, successful take of a specific section.

## Magnetic Timeline

TightEdit's timeline is designed for speed, featuring a dedicated **Tight Mode**.

- **Tight Mode (ON)**: Clips automatically snap together, closing any gaps as you delete content. This is perfect for talking-head videos and tutorials.
- **Freeform Mode**: For more creative control, you can switch to Freeform Mode to manually position clips and create intentional gaps.
- **Virtualized Canvas**: A high-performance, Metal-accelerated timeline ensures smooth scrolling even with thousands of clips.

## Picture-in-Picture (PiP)

TightEdit makes it easy to manage multi-cam setups, such as screen recordings combined with a face cam.

- **Direct Audio Sync**: Automatically align your face cam video and screen recording using waveform cross-correlation.
- **Per-Clip Settings**: Each clip on the timeline can have its own independent PiP configuration (Visible, Hidden, Position, Size, Shape).
- **Auto-Frame**: (Planned) AI-powered face detection ensures you always stay centered in your face cam overlay.

## Professional Export

Once your edit is complete, you can choose from several export options:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="High-Quality Video" icon="video">
    Export directly to MP4 or HEVC using hardware-accelerated **VideoToolbox** encoding. Choose from multiple quality presets (Draft, Standard, High, Maximum).
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="FCPXML Export" icon="share">
    Export your project as an **FCPXML** file to continue your edit in Final Cut Pro. All your cuts, tags, and basic PiP settings are preserved.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Transcript Export" icon="file-text">
    Export your edited transcript as a plain text file or SRT subtitles for social media captions.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
