The **Thumbnail Automation Tool** is built on a research-informed "Playbook" that defines core archetypes for high-performance YouTube thumbnails.

## Core Archetypes

### 1. Talking-Head Reaction
**Goal:** Establish a direct human connection and signal the emotional tone of the video.
- **Achievement:** High CTR through "mirroring".
- **Composition:** Large, expressive face (~40-60% of frame), Rule of Thirds.
- **Visual Cues:** Exaggerated expressions, bright face lighting.

### 2. Software/UI Close-Up
**Goal:** Show exactly what the viewer will learn or use.
- **Achievement:** Establishes credibility and utility.
- **Composition:** High-contrast crop of UI, glowing borders, mouse cursor focal point.

### 3. Comparison Split
**Goal:** Create immediate "A vs B" tension.
- **Achievement:** Triggers "which one is better?" curiosity.
- **Composition:** Vertical/diagonal split, high contrast (Green vs Red).

### 4. Before/After
**Goal:** Demonstrate a transformation or process result.
- **Achievement:** Proves value and creates desire.
- **Composition:** Left (Before) vs Right (After), brightness/color delta.

### 5. Object-Led Curiosity
**Goal:** Focus on a single, mysterious, high-value item.
- **Achievement:** High "Click-to-Find-Out" factor.
- **Composition:** Rim lighting, clean/dark background, mysterious subject.

### 6. Process/Workflow Reveal
**Goal:** Show complexity or "behind the scenes" expertise.
- **Achievement:** Signals deep value and effort.
- **Composition:** Layered elements, clear focal result, depth of field.

## Composition Heuristics

| Heuristic | Guidance |
|-----------|----------|
| **Rule of Thirds** | Place focal points at intersections of 3x3 grid. |
| **Contrast** | Ensure subject stands out from background. |
| **Safe Zones** | Avoid bottom-right corner (timestamp overlay). |
| **Color Palette** | High-vibrancy, complementary colors. |

## Prompt-Strategy Schema

The generation engine outputs a `prompt_strategy` object used for image generation:

- `archetype`: One of the 6 core strategies.
- `subject`: Primary focal point.
- `background`: Setting description.
- `framing`: Camera distance/angle.
- `lighting`: Rim light, studio, golden hour, etc.
- `text_overlay`: Maximum 4 words for legibility.

## Review Interface

- **Job Gallery**: View all generated variants for a job.
- **Side-by-Side**: Compare 2 variants for final selection.
- **Feedback Form**: Structured feedback for reruns (more contrast, bigger face, etc.).
