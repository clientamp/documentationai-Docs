---

## Overview

Capture a button click and send event data to Client Amp.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add Client Amp script">
    ````html
    <script src="https://cdn.lickityclick.com/client-amp.js" data-key="YOUR_KEY"></script>
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Mark a button to track">
    Add `data-lickity` to your button:
    ````html
    <button data-lickity="cta">Click Me</button>
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test in dashboard">
    Click your button, then check your Click Events page in Lickity Click.
  </Step>
</Steps>
