TightEdit is a high-performance, native video editor reimagined for the modern content creator. While traditional editors (NLEs) are designed for cinematic filmmaking, TightEdit is built from the ground up to solve the specific challenges of YouTubers, streamers, and podcasters.

## The Text-Based Revolution

TightEdit's core innovation is **Text-Based Editing**. By treating your video like a document, you can edit your footage by simply interacting with the transcript.

- **Delete Text to Delete Video**: Highlight and delete a sentence in the transcript, and the corresponding video is instantly removed from the timeline.
- **Auto-Silence Removal**: TightEdit automatically identifies and removes dead air, saving you hours of manual cutting.
- **Bad Take Detection**: AI-powered analysis identifies repeated phrases and "bad takes," allowing you to keep only your best delivery.

## Privacy & Performance

TightEdit is a **100% native Swift** application that leverages the full power of Apple Silicon.

- **On-Device AI**: Transcription is handled locally using **WhisperKit**, and silence detection uses **Core ML** Voice Activity Detection (VAD). Your raw footage and transcripts never leave your Mac.
- **Hardware Acceleration**: Video processing and encoding are powered by **Metal**, **AVFoundation**, and **VideoToolbox** for lightning-fast exports.
- **Native Experience**: A true macOS app that respects system conventions, keyboard shortcuts, and power management.
