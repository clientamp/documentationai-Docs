TightShare provides a professional, native macOS environment for content orchestration and distribution.

## CMS Publishing

The primary goal of TightShare is to streamline the publishing process from video to the web.

- **Direct Ghost CMS Integration**: Seamlessly publish your generated content to Ghost with support for posts, tags, and all essential metadata.
- **Rich Content Support**: Automate the creation and embedding of images, videos, and properly formatted HTML/Markdown content.
- **Status Control**: Manage your content as drafts or publish them immediately to your site.

## AI Orchestration

TightShare's transformation engine is powered by advanced AI orchestration.

- **Anthropic Integration**: Uses the **Anthropic API** for state-of-the-art content refinement and generation.
- **Intelligent Prompting**: The built-in `PromptBuilder` uses your video's transcript and metadata to generate context-aware blog posts that sound like you.
- **Workflow Orchestrator**: Manages the multi-stage pipeline from transcription to final draft, ensuring each step of the process is handled efficiently.

<Callout kind="info" title="BYOK (Bring Your Own Key)">
  TightShare follows a "Bring Your Own Key" model. You supply your own Anthropic API key, giving you full control over your AI costs and processing limits.
</Callout>

## Workflow Management

Automate the most repetitive parts of post-production.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Transcription Fetching" icon="download">
    Automatically retrieve high-quality transcripts from your YouTube videos to serve as the foundation for your content.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Content Refinement" icon="pencil">

  <Step title="Affiliate Integration" icon="link">

  <Step title="One-Click Publishing" icon="share">

  </Step>

## Tracked Affiliate Links

TightShare is uniquely designed for creators who monetize through affiliate marketing.

- **LickityClick Support**: Connect your LickityClick account to automatically create short, tracked URLs for any product mentioned in your video.
- **ROI Proving**: Use these tracked links to show sponsors exactly how much value your blog content is generating.

## SwiftData Storage

TightShare uses **SwiftData** to provide a robust, local-first experience on your Mac.

- **Offline Support**: Manage your projects and drafts even when you don't have an internet connection.
- **High Performance**: SwiftData ensures a responsive UI, even as your project library grows.
- **Automatic Sync**: Projects are automatically persisted, so you never lose your work.

<Callout kind="success" title="Native Performance">
  Built as a true Swift application, TightShare leverages the latest macOS frameworks for a smooth, high-performance experience.
</Callout>
